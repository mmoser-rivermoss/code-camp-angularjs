Scope Hierarchy

When the application is bootstrapped, the $rootScope is created. It is the parent of all the other scope instances.

  • $rootScope
    • scope001
    • scope002
      • scope004
      • scope005
        • scope008
        • scope009
    • scope003
      • scope006
      • scope007

Scopes will have a parent-child relationship. $scope takes on prototypical inheritance, which means that if a property is defined on the $scope, then it is seen by its children as long as a child somewhere along the way doesn't redefine it.
